My forms

Forms are saved in this browser only — nothing on any server. Open one to edit, create a new one, or import from a text file.

Import a form

Paste a form definition or upload a file. Two formats are accepted: indented (human-friendly) or JSON.

Format example 
title: Pre-appointment intake
description: Please fill before your visit

# Patient Information
text*    half    Full name
date*    half    Date of birth
text     half    Phone
text     half    Email

# Reason for Visit
textarea*        Primary reason for visit
yesno            Have you been treated for this before?
checkbox         Do you have any of these conditions?
                 - Diabetes
                 - Hypertension
                 - Heart disease
                 - Asthma

* after the type marks the field required. half pairs the field on a row with the next half-width field. Section headers start with #. Choices for radio/checkbox go on indented - option lines.

Private by design. ProxForm is GDPR-compliant by architecture — answers travel browser-to-browser, end-to-end encrypted. Like a sheet of paper, not a database.
