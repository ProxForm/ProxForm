How this works

Build → share two things → patient fills

The form lives in this browser only. When you're ready, generate an invite link and a one-time passphrase, then share each through a different channel. The patient's browser connects directly to yours — answers travel peer-to-peer, end-to-end encrypted. Nothing touches a server.

  1. 1

    Build

    Add the questions you need. The live preview on the right shows exactly what the patient will see.

  2. 2

    Generate

    Click Generate link. ProxForm creates an encrypted invite link and a random passphrase.

  3. 3

    Share two ways

    Send the link by email or chat. Send the passphrase by SMS or phone. The two-channel split is the security property.

  4. 4

    Receive

    The patient's browser connects directly to yours. Answers stream in live; submissions stay on this device only.
